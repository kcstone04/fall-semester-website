Fiction for Young People

Class Information

This class is about reading and writing anything from children's board books to young adult fiction. We read snippets of novels, board books, manga, and more in class, then discuss it.

Major Assignments

  • Presentation
  • Workshop Portfolio
  • Book Fair & Festival

My Thoughts on the Class

This is a more specialized creative writing class, but I find it to also be fairly chill and relaxing. The class is largely open discussion, and I know many of my fellow classmates from previous classes and organizations. Personally I have little interest in board books, but manga and YA novels are up my alley, so the class is overall very interesting to me.

Khadijah Queen

Dr. Queen has published six books, as well as a play which won the Leslie Scalapino Award for Innovative Women's Performance Writing. She has also written a selection of individual poems that appear in various other publications, including American Poetry Review and Buzzfeed. She now teaches creative writing at Virginia Tech.
