Creative Writing: Fiction

Class Information

This class is about writing creative works of fiction. The main focus of the class is writing workshops, wherein we read our classmates' pieces, then discuss them in class. Eventually, your own piece will also be workshopped, providing you with a wealth of feedback which can be used to improve it.

Major Assignments

  • Workshop Submissions
  • Writing Journal
  • Class Reflection Essay

My Thoughts on the Class

This class is very relaxing and chill to be in. I have taken this exact same class before, with the exact same professor. I enjoyed it the first time, and I am enjoying it this time as well. There are more people than there were previously, so there is more peer feedback to be had. I also love having a space to share and build upon my creative works.

Jeff Mann

Professor Mann has published six books of poetry and six novels. His studies focus on LGBTQ+ literature and appalachian literature - both the result of his personal life and upbringing. He has won two Lambda Literary Awards and four National Leather Association-International literary awards. Currently, he teaches creative writing at Virginia Tech, both poetry and fiction.
